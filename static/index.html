Tunisian AI Radiology Project

Tunisian AI Radiology Project is a new artificial intelligence (AI) tool that claims to detect COVID-19 by recognising images of a simple X-ray or CT scan of the chest. This tool is a part of the international efforts to build and train an AI model allowing a faster detection of COVID-19 patients. It is intended to support healthcare specialists managing COVID-19 cases.
